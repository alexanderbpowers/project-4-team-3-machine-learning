About Us

The News Reliability Checker is a machine learning application that reads online news articles and predicts whether the article is trustworthy or not. To do this, we trained a Na√Øve Bayes model using a fake news dataset that had previously evaluated the trustworthiness of 1000s of news articles.

To use the app, simply paste the URL of any news article into the input bar and click Analyse. The result will tell you whether or not the article can be trusted.
